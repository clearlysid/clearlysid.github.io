Ways of the Waves

I created a book cover for my hypothetical project about music-making technology and it's evolution from a financially punishing lifelihood to the modern bedroom music studio.

Wanderlust Magazine

While preserving its information hierarchy, I redesigned the cover of a Wanderlust Magazine issue. In the process, I understood how Information Architecture works on a small scale and also used my understanding of the basic design principles to fix some issues in the original cover.

A "Lifeline" for your squad

I composed a variety of interesting layouts starting with random cutouts from discarded magazines. Eventually picking one, and turned the layout into a large wall poster.

Tri-fold Brochure for "Android"

Android is a free geometric sans-serif typeface. I created a small leaflet which showcases all of its characters and features.
