Yet another expense tracker...

Despite all of the brilliant offerings on the market, I was intrigued by how most of the teen demographic in the country didn't use any of them. In this project, I set out to find the reasons why...and hopefully fix them if I could.

User Research

I surveyed about 120 College and High-School students and conducted 6 in-person interviews to further understand what barrier existed between their expenses and their tech.
